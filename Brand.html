About C-Class 2023

Like many a carmaker these days, Mercedes-Benz is largely pursuing a strategy of offering the same flavor of automobile in different sizes. The new all-electric EQE and EQS look almost identical, and you can bet their future SUV siblings will as well; the GT and GT 4-Door could be confused for one another from the front if you didn't have them side-by-side for reference. It's even present in their naming strategy; there's a reason Mercedes rebranded the GLK-, ML- and GL-Class SUVs as GLC, GLE and GLS, and it ain't because their badge supplier was offering a deal on the letters G and L. About Maybach 2023

The current generation of Mercedes-Benz Maybach S-Class was introduced in 2021 alongside the standard seventh-generation S-Class. It combines the cutting-edge tech features of Mercedes' newest flagship with handcrafted luxury touches that elevate it to another level of premium. For 2023, changes to the Maybach are minor. There's a new Manufaktur line of exclusive paint and exterior trim colors and two limited-production special editions: the Haute Voiture and S 680 by Virgil Abloh. The Maybach offers two engine choices, including a 496-horsepower twin-turbocharged V8 and a 621-hp twin-turbo V12. They're considered different trim levels, but each offers the same generous list of standard features. The Mercedes-Benz Maybach comes truly loaded, with features like a massive 12.8-inch digital instrument panel, a 12.3-inch OLED touchscreen infotainment system, a full-color head-up display, massaging front seats, rear neck heaters and a 30-speaker audio system all included. Mercedes has traditionally used the S-Class to show off its latest and greatest features, and the 2023 Maybach showcases the very best the three-pointed star brand has to offer. The Maybach is one of only a handful of cars competing in the ultra-luxe full-size sedan class. Its two top rivals are the Bentley Flying Spur and Rolls-Royce Ghost, but you might also cross-shop it with a high-dollar spec of the regular S-Class. They'll drive similarly, but the Maybach offers an air of exclusivity that must be experienced to be fully understood
